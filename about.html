CHURCHILL MAJANI BIO

Churchill Majani is the founding father of The Little Taco shop.He was born in 1985 in a middle class family.He however always dreamed of starting a business and nurturing it till it gained International Status.After brainstorming,He came up with the idea of starting a Taco business as it was not yet established in Kenya.It was difficult at the beginning but through sheer hardwork and good judgements,the business boomed to the point he could not run it alone.Coupling this with the growing demand,He had to employ others for help and thus created job opportunities.It has thus grown over the years and attained the International status.We thus hope that you will join us and enjoy the life-changing tacos
